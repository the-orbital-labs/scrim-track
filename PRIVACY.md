# ScrimTrack Privacy Policy

ScrimTrack is a Chrome extension that helps Scrimba learners track learning activity, study time, streaks, and progress.

## Data collected

ScrimTrack may collect and store the following data locally in your browser:

- Learning activity on Scrimba
- Time spent learning
- Streak and progress data
- Scrimba page/course/lesson information needed to track progress

## How data is stored

ScrimTrack stores data locally in your browser using Chrome storage. The data is not sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

## Remote servers

ScrimTrack does not send your learning activity, progress, or website content to external servers.

## Third parties

ScrimTrack does not sell or transfer user data to third parties.

## Contact

For questions or support, please open an issue on the ScrimTrack GitHub repository.
